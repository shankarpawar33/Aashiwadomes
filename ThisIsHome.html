This Is Home – Aashirwad Homes

A home is a place where you sleep every night, a place you always return to like a homing pigeon. A place where you feel safe, feel yourself, feel happy and always feel loved. To us THIS IS HOME …and this is what we build.


Have you ever had that ‘Yipee I’m home’feeling when you open your house door after many days of vacationing? A vacation where you were pampered by courteous hotel staff, where you stayed in the best hotels with the most luxurious rooms? Nothing quite beats the comfort your beloved couch or the beauty sleep you get on your own bed or even the feeling of strutting around in your comfy clothes. All because this is your own home. You are the king of the world in your space.


If only four walls and ‘things’ made a house a home; then no one would remember those precious memories where children chased each other around in nothing but a vest and underwear with a kitchen ‘kadhai’ doubling up as a soldier’s helmet. The aroma of your favourite ‘sabzi’ floating in from the kitchen while you’re getting the TV fixed (after many reminders) or even those lazy Sunday afternoons where you ate breakfast at lunch and lunch got pushed to tea time.


All such memories begin as a dream in the eyes of a home buyer. Haven’t we all experienced this feeling when visiting a project? The minute you step into the sample flat, you start making a mental picture of how own house and then you start imagining your life there. Dreaming about making it your own home one day.


We at Aarshiwaad Homes know and believe that homes are not made on the foundations of concrete and cement, they are made up of millions of hopes and wishes that people have. Anyone who is looking to buying a home in Pune is dreaming of creating a special place for himself and his family - their own home. Homes are made when people’s dreams come true and they can say – This is my home.


Aashirwad Homes in Ravet is a property crafted with love to be your little haven in the cushions of this green earth. These 2 BHK premium homes assure highest quality so that your family can feel safe. A strategic location that does not keep you away from your family for too long when you have to commute. A community living where your family can meet and explore like-minded people from different cultural backgrounds. Square-feet of open green spaces for you to play, breathe fresh air and live in.


At Aashirwad Homes, Ravet we’ve created the four walls but it’s time for you and your family to bring them to life by fulfilling all your aspirations of living in your own home. In our modular kitchens you can live your fantasy of being a master chef. On the private terraces and garden sitting areas rediscover deep conversations that may have been put on hold for too long. At our play areas and indoor game areas children can build everlasting friendships with other kids rather than being attached to screens and video games. We have create a space for many families to come together and make one big family who celebrate special occasions and festivals at the club-house and common garden areas. Buying a home is not about putting your life’s savings into a concrete asset. It’s about finding a space that you can truly call your home. Finding those solid four walls which you can paint with a lifetime of


memories, a place where you can breathe sigh of relief not just once but every day for the rest of your life. A place to step into and say ‘This Is Home’ .
